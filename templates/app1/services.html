Yashoda Hospitals is a chain of hospitals based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It has branches in Somajiguda, Secunderabad, and Malakpet with another branch coming up in Hitec City. It was recognised as a leading hospital for oncology in 2015 after becoming the first hospital to achieve the milestone of treating 1,00,000+ patients using RapidArc technology.
