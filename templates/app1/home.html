Welcome to Yashoda Hospital

Yashoda Hospitals is a chain of hospitals based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It has branches in Somajiguda, Secunderabad, and Malakpet with another branch coming up in Hitec City. All of its branches are NABH and NABL accredited. It was recognised as a leading hospital for oncology in 2014 after becoming the first hospital to achieve the milestone of treating 30,000+ patients using Rapid Arc technology.
